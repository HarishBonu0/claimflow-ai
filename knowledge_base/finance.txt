Money and Claims - Simple Guide

Understanding Deductible

English:
Deductible is the money YOU pay when you file a claim.

Simple example:
Your deductible: $500
Damage costs: $2000
YOU pay: $500
Insurance pays: $1500

Choose deductible:
Higher deductible = Lower monthly fee
Lower deductible = Higher monthly fee

Most people choose: $500 or $1000

यदि आपका डिडक्टिबल $500 है और नुकसान $2000 है,
तो आप $500 देते हैं और कंपनी $1500 देती है।

Coverage Limits

English:
Coverage limit is the maximum amount insurance pays.

Example:
Your limit: $10,000
Damage cost: $15,000
Insurance pays: $10,000 (not more)
You pay difference: $5,000

Types of limits:
1. Per claim limit: Max for one incident
2. Annual limit: Max for whole year
3. Category limit: Max for specific type

Important: Check your policy limits!

Co-insurance and Co-payment

English:
Co-insurance means you and company share the cost.

Example of 80-20 co-insurance:
Total damage: $1000
Insurance pays: 80% = $800
YOU pay: 20% = $200

Co-payment is fixed amount:
Example: $50 for doctor visit
Does not change.

Common in health insurance.

Claim Settlement Calculation

English:
Here is how payment is calculated:

Step 1: Find damage cost
Get repair estimate or medical bills.

Step 2: Check policy
Does policy cover this?
What is limit?

Step 3: Subtract deductible
$2000 - $500 = $1500

Step 4: Check limit
Cannot exceed limit.

Step 5: Apply co-insurance
If any.

Step 6: Final amount

Example:
Cost: $3000
Deductible: $500
After deductible: $2500
Limit: $2000
Final payment: $2000

Monthly Payment (Premium)

English:
Premium is money you pay monthly for insurance.

Premium depends on:
Type of insurance.
Deductible amount.
Coverage limits.
Your risk level.
Your past claims.

Payment options:
Monthly (automatic bank transfer)
Quarterly (4 times per year)
Yearly (once per year)

IMPORTANT: If you miss payment, insurance stops!

Payment Timeline

English:
How long do you wait for money?

Small claims (under $1000):
Wait: 1-2 weeks

Medium claims ($1000-$5000):
Wait: 2-4 weeks

Large claims (over $5000):
Wait: 4-8 weeks

Fastest way: Direct bank transfer (2-3 days)
Slowest way: Check by mail (5-10 days)

Types of Claims and Money

English:
Different claims get different payment:

Health Claim:
Doctor visit: $50 co-pay
Hospital: Deductible + co-insurance
Medicine: % of cost

Car Claim:
Deductible applies.
Repair cost covered.
Max limit applies.

Home Claim:
Deductible applies.
Furniture covered.
Jewelry has limit.

Life Claim:
Full amount to family.
No deductible.
Usually fast (7-30 days).

Subrogation (Getting Money Back)

English:
If another person caused damage, insurance can get money back from them.

Example:
Car B hits your car.
You claim, get $2000.
Insurance pays you $2000.
Insurance sues car B.
Gets money from car B.
Money helps keep costs low for everyone.

You don't do anything - insurance handles it.

Hindi:
